Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the emergency alert work?

    Press and hold the ring for three seconds. SafeRing instantly sends your live location and an SOS message to your chosen emergency contacts.

  • Is SafeRing waterproof?

    Yes. SafeRing is water-resistant up to 50 meters, so it holds up through showers, workouts, and swimming.

  • How long does the battery last?

    A full charge powers SafeRing for up to seven days of everyday use, including health monitoring and standby alerting.

  • Can I use SafeRing with any smartphone?

    SafeRing pairs over Bluetooth with the SafePulse app, available for both iOS and Android devices.

  • Is my data secure?

    All location and health data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and it's only ever shared with the contacts you choose.
